24. Responsive Design

What is it?

Responsive design means that the layout of a webpage autmatically adjusts to the size and capabilitys of the users device. For example, if you look at this page from your laptop, these three boxes will appear horisontically aligned. But if you look at it from your phone (or shrink your browser to less then 600 px), the boxes will align vertically.

Why use it?

By using responsive design you can make a webpage look different depending on whether its beeing viewed from a laptop, tablet or a phone. Here are some examples. This makes the webpage more user friendly and also saves the developer a lot of time and work, instead of having to code every version of a website seperatly.
